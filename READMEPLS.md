# ♟️ Kerbal Chess Engine v10

**Kerbal v10** is a high-performance chess engine focused on stronger decision-making, deeper evaluation, and continuous AI improvement.

> ⚠️ Note: All versions **below v10** were created solely by the original developer. Kerbal v10 represents a major evolution of the engine.

---

## 🚀 Overview

Kerbal v10 is designed to:

* Think **deeper**
* Evaluate positions **smarter**
* Play **stronger and more human-like chess**

This version introduces major upgrades to the AI core, improving how the engine analyzes positions and selects moves.

---

## 🧠 AI Improvements in v10

Kerbal v10 focuses heavily on **better thinking**, not just faster moves.

### Key Enhancements:

* **Improved Evaluation Function**

  * Better piece value balancing
  * Smarter positional understanding
  * Enhanced king safety logic

* **Deeper Search**

  * Optimized move ordering
  * More efficient pruning (cuts bad moves faster)
  * Increased search depth without massive slowdown

* **Smarter Decision-Making**

  * Reduced blunders
  * Better tactical awareness
  * Improved handling of complex positions

* **Future-Ready AI Design**

  * Built to allow:

    * Machine learning integration (future)
    * Opening book expansion
    * Endgame tablebase support
